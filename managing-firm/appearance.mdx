Byron supports light and dark modes so you can pick the look that's easiest on your eyes — or let it follow your operating system automatically.

<Note>
  Appearance settings are rolling out gradually. If you don't see the **Appearance** tab in **Settings**, it hasn't been enabled for your firm yet — reach out to [support@usebyron.com](mailto:support@usebyron.com) to have it turned on.
</Note>

## Choosing a theme

To change how Byron looks:

1. Open **Settings** from the account menu.
2. Go to the **Appearance** tab.
3. Pick one of the three options:
   - **System** — Byron follows your operating system's light or dark setting and updates automatically when you change it.
   - **Light** — always use the light theme.
   - **Dark** — always use the dark theme.

Your selection is saved to your browser and persists across reloads. If you set the theme to **System**, Byron will switch automatically when your OS toggles between light and dark — no reload required.

## What changes with the theme

The theme affects Byron's interface chrome — tabs, tables, sidebars, status badges, the document list, and the extraction review panes. Document previews and PDF content render the same in both modes.

## Troubleshooting

- **The theme didn't stick after I logged back in.** The preference is stored per browser. If you sign in from a different browser or device, set your preference again.
- **My OS is in dark mode but Byron is still light.** Check that **Appearance** is set to **System** rather than **Light**.
