title: "Removing users"
description: "When job is done, you can easily remove user access from your organization in Byron."
---

## To remove a user from your organization:

1. Navigate to Team Members
2. Find the user in the list
3. Click the three-dot menu next to their name
4. Select "Remove from Organization"
5. Confirm the removal

<Note>
  **Important:** Removing a user immediately revokes their access. Any in-progress work should be reassigned before removal
</Note>
