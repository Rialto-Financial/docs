title: "Managing team members"
description: "Add new users to your firm and send invite emails."
---

Invite your team members to your organization in Byron so you can collaborate on tax returns.

<Note>
  **Note:** You must be an **organization administrator** to be able to invite new team members or manage access of existing users
</Note>

1. Click on your organization name in the top right corner
2. Select "Team Members" from the dropdown menu
3. Click the "Invite User" button

### **Step 2: Send Invitations**

For each team member you want to invite:

1. Enter their email address
2. Select their role:
   - **Admin:** Full access to all features, settings, and user management
   - **User:** Can upload documents, review extractions, make corrections, and export data
   - **Viewer:** Read-only access to projects and documents
3. Optionally add a personal message
4. Click "Send Invitation"

### **Step 3: Manage User Permissions**

After users accept their invitations, you can further customize their permissions:

| **Permission** | **Admin** | **User** | **Viewer** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Upload documents | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Review extractions | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Make corrections | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Export data | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Manage integrations | ✓ | — | — |
| Invite users | ✓ | — | — |

### **User Invitation Status**

Track invitation status from the Team Members page:

- **Pending:** Invitation sent but not yet accepted
- **Active:** User has accepted and can access the platform
- **Suspended:** User access temporarily disabled

<Note>
  **Tip:** Resend invitations that are pending for more than 48 hours. Check spam folders if users report not receiving invitations.
</Note>
