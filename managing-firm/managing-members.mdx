## How to add or remove a team member today

To invite a new team member, remove someone, or change someone's access, email [support@usebyron.com](mailto:support@usebyron.com). In your message, include:

- The full name and email address of the person being added, removed, or updated.
- What you'd like done (add to firm, remove from firm, change access level).
- Your firm name, so we can route the request to the right account.

Our support team will action the change and confirm back to you right away.
