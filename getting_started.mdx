### Step 2: **Configure Organization Settings**

Once your organization is created, configure the following settings:

- **Firm information:** Enter your firm's EIN, licensing information, and jurisdiction details
- **Branding:** Upload your firm logo and customize color schemes for client-facing materials
- **Default workflows:** Set up standard processing workflows for different return types
- **Notification preferences:** Configure how and when team members receive alerts

<Note>
  **Best Practice:** Complete your organization profile thoroughly during initial setup. This information will be used across the platform and in client communications.
</Note>

### Step 3: Invite users

Add team members to your Byron organization so they can access the platform and collaborate on tax returns.

<Accordion title="How to add and manage users in your organization">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Step 1: Navigate to User Management">
      1. Click on your organization name in the top right corner
      2. Select "Team Members" from the dropdown menu
      3. Click the "Invite User" button
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 2: Enter the email address for each team member you want to invite:">
      1. Enter their email address
      2. Select their role:
         - **Admin:** Full access to all features, settings, and user management
         - **User:** Can upload documents, review extractions, make corrections, and export data
         - **Viewer:** Read-only access to projects and documents
      3. Optionally add a personal message
      4. Click "Send Invitation"
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 3: Manage User Permissions">
      |  | **Admin** | **User** | **Viewer** |
      | --- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
      | Upload documents | ✓ | ✓ | — |
      | Review extractions | ✓ | ✓ | — |
      | Make corrections | ✓ | ✓ | — |
      | Export data | ✓ | ✓ | — |
      | Manage integrations | ✓ | — | — |
      | Invite users | ✓ | — | — |
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
