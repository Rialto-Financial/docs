## Purpose-built for tax forms

Byron's AI is trained specifically on tax documents. It understands the structure of W-2s, 1099s, K-1s, Schedule Cs, and composite statements, including where data lives across face sheets, footnotes, and supplemental schedules. That's what gets Byron to 98–99% accuracy on fields that generic tools routinely miss.

For K-1s in particular, Byron doesn't stop at the face sheet. It reads the footnotes and pulls out detailed breakouts — Section 199A by activity, state K-1 detail, K-3 data — the kind of information that typically takes a preparer 15–30 minutes to transcribe by hand.

## Confidence scoring on every field

Byron doesn't just extract data — it scores how confident it is in every value it reads.

- **High confidence** fields are marked as ready. Byron is sure about these, and you can trust them at a glance.
- **Review** fields are surfaced for you to check. These are values Byron isn't certain about, usually because the source document is unclear, unusual, or poorly scanned.

This means you don't need to re-verify every number. You focus your attention only where it's actually needed, and move through high-confidence data in seconds.

## Human in the loop

Byron is designed to keep your team in control. Every extracted value can be reviewed, edited, approved, or rejected. **Anchors** show exactly where each number came from on the source PDF, so verification is fast — one click and you're looking at the spot Byron pulled it from.

Every action is tracked in a full audit log: who approved what, who corrected what, and when. For firms, that means seasonal preparers, senior reviewers, and partners can all work in the same system with a clear record of who did what.

## Roll forward intelligence

If your firm has prior year data, Byron uses it. When this year's documents come in, Byron matches them to the client's expected documents from last year automatically — so nothing slips through and nothing gets duplicated.

New to Byron? No prior data needed. You create a new client, upload documents, and Byron handles the rest.

## Built for how firms actually work

Multiple team members can work on the same client at the same time. Permissions, approval states, and audit trails are designed for real firm workflows — so the work of a seasonal preparer, a senior reviewer, and a partner all live in one system without stepping on each other.
