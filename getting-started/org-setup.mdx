If you're the first person at your firm to use Byron, this page walks through the one-time setup. Most firms can complete it in under 10 minutes. Once setup is done, your firm is ready to upload its first document.

The setup is four steps:

1. Create your firm account
2. Connect your tax preparation software
3. Set up other integrations, such as practice management software, storage, etc.
4. Invite your team

<Note>
  You can skip any step and come back to it later. The only thing you need before processing your first document is a firm account. If you've already received an invite from a colleague, see [Joining your firm on Byron](/getting-started/joining-your-firm) instead.
</Note>

## Step 1: Create your firm account

Go to [app.usebyron.com](https://app.usebyron.com) and sign up. During signup you'll be asked for:

- **Firm name** — what your team and clients will see across Byron.
- **Primary contact** — name and email of the person managing the account.
- **Tax preparation software** — what your firm uses to prepare returns (Lacerte, CCH Axcess, GoSystem, or Other). You can change this later.

<Note>
  Every Byron account can export to a **Full Workpaper** (Excel) and a **Collated PDF**, regardless of which tax software you select. A direct tax-software integration is optional, not required.
</Note>

Once your firm is created, you'll land on your dashboard.

## Step 2: Connect your tax preparation software

Byron integrates with your existing tools to create a seamless workflow from client onboarding through tax filing. Setup is a one-time configuration per platform.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lacerte" icon="gear" iconType="regular">
    Lacerte integration uses the Data Conductor import system for seamless data transfer.

    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Lacerte**
    2. Click **Configure Lacerte export**
    3. Download the Lacerte configuration file
    4. Open Lacerte and import the file following the on-screen instructions
    5. Run a test export from Byron to confirm the connection

    See the [Lacerte  guide](/exporting/lacerte) for the full in-depth walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="CCH Axcess" icon="gear-complex">
    Byron to CCH Axcess connection can be established in multiple ways, including through OAuth authentication and API connections if your organization has a dedicated CCH Axcess API. You'll need your CCH account number and admin access in CCH.

    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → CCH Axcess**
    2. Click **Connect CCH Axcess**
    3. Enter your CCH account number
    4. Sign in and authorize Byron when CCH prompts you
    5. Confirm the connection status shows **Connected**

    <Note>
      If authorization fails, your CCH administrator may need to enable API access for your account. Contact CCH support if you're stuck.
    </Note>

    See the [CCH Axcess  guide](/exporting/cch-axcess) for the full in-depth walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Something else" icon="envelope">
    If you use a tax platform not listed here, [email us](mailto:support@usebyron.com) — we're adding integrations based on customer demand. UltraTax and Drake are coming soon.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Always run a test export before tax season opens. Catching a misconfiguration and fixing it ahead of time is a lot easier than doing it during a busy week.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Set up other integrations

To allow your organization to manage its workflow seamlessly, Byron also supports other integrations. As an administrator for your organization, you can configure and manage these integrations by following the steps below as needed.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Practice Management Integration - Karbon">
    Connect Karbon to sync client records, work items, and task status.

    1. Navigate to Settings \> Integrations \> Karbon
    2. Click "Connect Karbon"
    3. Enter your Karbon organization URL
    4. Authorize the connection with your Karbon credentials
    5. Select which workflows should sync to Byron
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Document Storage Integration - Dropbox">
    Link Dropbox for automatic document ingestion and storage.

    1. Navigate to Settings \> Integrations \> Dropbox
    2. Click "Connect Dropbox"
    3. Authorize Acacia AI to access your Dropbox
    4. Select the folders that contain client tax documents
    5. Configure automatic sync settings

    <Note>
      Ensure your Dropbox folder structure is organized by client or return year for optimal automatic processing.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Client Portal Integration - Truss">
    Connect Truss for automated client document collection and e-signatures.

    1. Navigate to Settings \> Integrations \> Truss
    2. Click "Connect Truss"
    3. Enter your Truss API credentials
    4. Map Truss projects to Acacia AI client records
    5. Configure document upload notifications
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Card title="Integration Status Dashboard" icon="traffic-light">
  After configuring integrations, monitor their status from the Integrations Dashboard:

  - **Green indicator:** Integration is active and syncing
  - **Yellow indicator:** Integration requires attention or re-authentication
  - **Red indicator:** Integration has failed and needs reconfiguration
</Card>

## Step 4: Invite your team

Add the people who'll be uploading, reviewing, or approving documents this season.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click on your organization name  ">
    Find the organization settings in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage or invite members">
    Select  "Team Members" from the dropdown menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Enter the person's email and assign a role. They'll receive an invite email with a link to set up their account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For what each role can and can't do and further info on user management, see [Managing team members](/managing-firm/managing-members).
