Setting up your firm on Byron today is a hands-on process led by our team. Most of the technical setup happens behind the scenes — the only part that requires active participation from your firm is the integration with your tax preparation software. This page walks through what to expect and what we'll need from you along the way.

## What Byron handles

Our engineering team takes care of the foundational setup work for your firm:

- **Creating your organization in Byron** — the workspace your team will work in.
- **Provisioning the initial admin account** — the first user with administrator access who can later coordinate inviting the rest of your team.
- **Configuring environment-level settings** — the technical defaults that determine how your firm's instance of Byron operates.

You don't need to take any action for these steps. We'll provide your administrator's login details once the account is ready.

## Where we will work together to get things up and running

The area that requires your firm's involvement is connecting Byron to your tax preparation software. This is a coordinated session between your firm and our engineering team, scheduled at a time that works for both sides.

For each integration, the process typically involves:

- Confirming the tax software your firm uses and the version installed.
- Working with our team to authenticate the connection — for example, configuring API access for CCH Axcess, or setting up the import profile inside Data Conductor for Lacerte.
- Running a test export to confirm the integration works end to end.

You'll need someone on your team with administrator access to your tax software for this part — typically your firm's IT lead or whoever manages your tax software setup day to day.

## After setup is complete

Once your organization is set up and your tax software integration is verified, your administrator can move on to the rest of the workflow:

- **Bring in your client base** from your tax software — see [Setting up your client base](/managing-clients/client-base).
- **Add team members** to your firm in Byron — currently coordinated through our support team. See [Managing team members](/managing-firm/managing-members).
- **Process your first filing** — see [Quick start: your first document](/getting-started/first-document).

## Getting started

To kick off setup, reach out to your CSM, or email [support@usebyron.com](mailto:support@usebyron.com). We'll schedule the integration session and let you know what to have ready in advance.
