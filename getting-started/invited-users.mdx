---
title: "Joining your firm on Byron"
---

If your firm administrator has already set up Byron and invited you to join, getting started takes only few minutes. The integrations, workflows, and team have already been configured for you — all you need to do is set up your account and find your way around the dashboard.

<Note>
  If you're the first person at your firm to use Byron — meaning you'll be setting up integrations and inviting teammates — see [Setting up your firm on Byron](/getting-started/org-setup) instead.
</Note>

## Accept your invite

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invite email">
    Look for an email from Byron with your firm's name in the subject line. If you can't find it, check your spam folder or ask your firm admin to resend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your account">
    Click the link in the email. You'll be asked to set a password and confirm a few basics like your name. Use the same email address the invite was sent to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to Byron">
    Go to [app.usebyron.com](https://app.usebyron.com) and sign in. You'll land directly on your firm's dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Get oriented on the dashboard

When you sign in for the first time, you'll see your firm's dashboard — the same view your teammates see. From here you can browse client filings, open a filing to upload or review documents, and switch between filings using the sidebar.
