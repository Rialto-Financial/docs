Setting up Byron takes only 4 steps which you can complete in less than 10 minutes. Once setup is complete, your firm is ready to upload its first document.

1. Create your Byron account
2. Configure your organization settings
3. Connect your tax preparation software
4. Invite your team

<Note>
  You'll need administrator access and login credentials for any tax software you plan to connect.

  You can skip any step and come back later. The only requirement before processing documents is creating your account.
</Note>

## Step 1: Create your firm account

Visit [app.usebyron.com](https://app.usebyron.com) and sign up. During signup, you'll be asked for a few basics:

- **Firm name** — what your team and clients will see across Byron
- **Primary contact** — name and email of the person managing the account
- **Tax preparation software** — Lacerte, CCH Axcess, UltraTax, or Other (you can change this later)

<Note>
  Every Byron account can export to **Excel work papers** and **collated PDF**, regardless of which tax software you select. A direct tax software integration is optional — not required — to use Byron.
</Note>

Once your firm is created, you'll land on your dashboard.

## Step 2: Configure your firm settings

From the dashboard, open **Settings → Firm** and complete the following. These details are reused across exports, audit logs, and team invites, so filling them in now saves time later.

- **Firm details** — firm name, EIN, jurisdiction, and licensing information
- **Branding** — upload your firm's logo to appear on exports and client-facing materials
- **Default workflows** — set the default approval and review behavior for your firm (you can override these per client)
- **Notifications** — choose how and when team members are alerted to new documents, completed extractions, or pending reviews

<Tip>
  If multiple people at your firm will be configuring settings, decide up front who owns notifications and default workflows. Inconsistent defaults cause confusion during peak season.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Connect your tax preparation software

Byron exports extracted data directly into your tax prep software — no reformatting, no manual re-entry. Setup is a one-time configuration per platform.

Open **Settings → Integrations** and follow the steps for your software.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lacerte" icon="gear">
    Byron exports to Lacerte using a configuration file you import once into Lacerte's Data Conductor. After that, Byron-generated exports drop straight into the right client return.

    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Lacerte**
    2. Click **Configure Lacerte export**
    3. Download the Lacerte configuration file
    4. Open Lacerte and import the file following the on-screen instructions
    5. Run a test export from Byron to confirm the connection

    See the [Lacerte export guide](/exporting/lacerte) for the full walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="CCH Axcess Tax" icon="gear-complex">
    Byron connects to CCH Axcess via OAuth. You'll need your CCH account number and admin access in CCH.

    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → CCH Axcess**
    2. Click **Connect CCH Axcess**
    3. Enter your CCH account number
    4. Sign in and authorize Byron when CCH prompts you
    5. Confirm the connection status shows **Connected**

    <Note>
      If authorization fails, your CCH administrator may need to enable API access for your account. Contact CCH support if you're stuck.
    </Note>

    See the [CCH Axcess export guide](/exporting/cch-axcess) for the full walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="UltraTax CS" icon="gear">
    Byron exports to UltraTax CS in a format ready to import directly into your client return.

    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → UltraTax**
    2. Click **Configure UltraTax export**
    3. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your UltraTax version
    4. Run a test export from Byron to confirm the connection

    See the [UltraTax export guide](/exporting/ultratax) for the full walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="GoSystem Tax" icon="gear">
    Byron exports to GoSystem Tax RS via an Excel workbook you import directly into GoSystem.

    1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → GoSystem**
    2. Click **Configure GoSystem export**
    3. Run a test export from Byron to confirm the connection

    See the [GoSystem export guide](/exporting/gosystem) for the full walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Drake Tax" icon="gear">

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Something else" icon="gear">

### Confirming your integration is working

Once connected, head to **Settings → Integrations** to check the status indicators:

- **Green** — the integration is active and ready to export
- **Yellow** — the integration needs attention (usually a re-authentication)
- **Red** — the integration has failed and needs to be reconfigured

<Tip>
  Run one test export before tax season opens. Catching a misconfiguration in January is a lot easier than catching it on April 10th.
</Tip>

## Step 4: Invite your team

You don't have to use Byron alone. Add the people who'll be uploading, reviewing, or approving documents during tax season.

1. Open the **organization switcher** in the sidebar
2. Select **Manage members** or **Invite members**
3. Enter their email and assign a role
4. They'll receive an invite email with a link to set up their account

Roles control what each person can see and do — for example, who can manage integrations and invite others versus who can upload and review.

For a full breakdown of roles and best practices for staffing, see [Adding your team](/getting-started/adding-team). If you're bringing on seasonal preparers, see [Onboarding seasonal staff](/getting-started/seasonal-staff).
